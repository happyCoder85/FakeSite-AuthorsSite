Breaking the Oath at Turner Creek

In the sleepy town of Turner Creek, a chilling oath is broken, unleashing a wave of terror that Detective Lin must unravel before it consumes the community.

When a prominent family is found brutally murdered, their bodies marked with a cryptic symbol, the tight-knit town descends into paranoia and suspicion. Detective Lin, a young, sharp-witted investigator with a troubled past, is called in to lead the case.

Haunted by his own demons, Lin must navigate the treacherous waters of small-town politics and long-held secrets to uncover the truth behind the killings. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dark history of betrayal and vengeance that threatens to destroy Turner Creek from within.

With time running out and the body count rising, Lin must race against the clock to break the code of the oath and expose the killer before they strike again.

In this gripping debut novel, Alex Williams weaves a suspenseful tale of murder, mystery, and the unbreakable bonds of family. With its richly drawn characters, atmospheric setting, and pulse-pounding plot, "Breaking the Oath at Turner Creek" will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very last page.
